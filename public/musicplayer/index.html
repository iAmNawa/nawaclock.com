Lana Del Rey

Here is some information about Elizabeth Woolridge Grant...

  1. 6/21/1985 Born
  2. Lana Del Rey sang in the church choir as a child.
  3. She had aspirations to become a poet.
  4. Her parents were fans of Carly Simon and The Beach boys, and her father wrote country songs while she was growing up.
  5. After high school, she went on to graduate from State University of New York at Geneseo.
  6. Instead of starting her singing career, she was a waitress on Long Island for a year.
  7. Living on Long Island, her Uncle taught her how to play guitar.
  8. Shortly after, she started performing songs in local dive bars under the names "Sparkle Jump Rope Queen" and "Lizzy Grant and the Phenomena."
  9. During this time, she went on to start attending Fordham University for Philosphy.
  10. There she volunteered helping the homeless and youth recover from addiction.
  11. She was also helping Indian Reservations by painting and building houses there.
  12. In 2005, she seriously started a music career as Lana Del Rey.
